How to get your session cookies
01Open Chrome and make sure you're logged into the publication.
02Visit the saved articles page (e.g. ft.com/myft/saved-articles/...).
03Open DevTools: ⌘ + Option + I, then go to the Network tab.
04Reload the page. Click the first request in the list (the HTML document).
05In the Headers tab, find Request Headers → cookie:. Copy the entire value.
06Paste it into the field above and click Save.
